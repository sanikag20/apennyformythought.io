    ABOUT ME

    Hey! I am Sanika Gumaste. I recently graduated from IIT Kanpur, and am planning to spend the next few years of my life in Pune. I would love to list down my hobbies, but they keep changing every other month. A few things that stay constant - I love reading fiction, watching and re-watching TV series and cleaning (yes, you read that right). I also love listening to music, so if you want to know me better- do listen to this. 

     

    I spend most of my time online- reading and watching any and all content that comes my way. Consider this a futile attempt to make my share. 

    ​

    I love to write. Be it investigative pieces, diaries, fanfictions or just random stories that seem to appear out of thin air. People say the way to someone's heart is through cooking. I say the way to people's minds is through writing. Hope this blog helps me reach out to your mind, dear reader.

    Wishing you a good read.
